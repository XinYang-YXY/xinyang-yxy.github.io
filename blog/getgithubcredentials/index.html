Quick guide to obtain your Google client credentials

  • Go to Github site
  • Go the the credential page
  • Configure and obtain credentials

Go to Github site

  1. Open the Github URL into your browser.
  2. Register an account and login.

Go the the credential page

  1. Click on the ‘profile logo’ on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Click on the ‘setting’.
  3. At the bottom of the left bar, click on ‘Developer Setting’.
  4. Click the ‘OAuth Apps’ on the left left bar.
  5. Click on the ‘New OAuth App’ button at the right hand area of the page.

Configure and obtain credentials

  1. Choose a name you like for the ‘Application Name’.
  2. Homepage URL is the domain of your app, in my case. I am using localhost. So mine looks like this
http://127.0.0.1:3000
  1. For ‘Authorization callback URL’. you need to put in similar like the following.
http://127.0.0.1:3000/auth/github/callback
  1. Click ‘Register application’ to get your credentials.
  2. DONE!
