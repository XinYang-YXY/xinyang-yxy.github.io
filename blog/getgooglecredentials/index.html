Quick guide to obtain your Google client credentials

  • Go to GCP [Google Cloud Platform]

Go to GCP [Google Cloud Platform]

  1. Open the GCP URL into your browser.
  2. Sign in to your GCP.
  3. Click on the console button on the top right part of the page.
  4. Tick the 2 boxes to agree if this is the first time you are accessing GCP.
  5. Find the burger button at the top left part of the page.
  6. Click on the 4th option called ‘APIs & Services’.
  7. Create a project if you didn’t.
  8. Select the project you created at the left hand side of the search bar.
  9. At the left bar, click on the credentials tab.
  10. Click the ‘+ CREATE CREDENTIALS’ button at the left hand side of the ‘DELETE’ button.
  11. A dropdown menu will be shown, select the second option called ‘OAuth client ID’.
  12. Click on the ‘CONFIGURE CONSENT SCREEN’ button. [If you see field asking for application type, please jump to step 15]
  13. User type is External.
  14. Type in a name in the Application name field, scroll down and click on the save button.
  15. Select ‘Web application’ as the Application type.
  16. Scroll down to ‘Authorised redirect URIs’.
  17. Click on ‘ADD URI’. Paste in
http://127.0.0.1:3000/auth/google/callback
  1. Create on ‘Create’.
  2. A modal should jump out.
  3. First part is your client ID, and the second part is your Client Secret.
  4. DONE!
